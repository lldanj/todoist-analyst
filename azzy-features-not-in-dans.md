
## Productivity & Scoring

- **Week-over-week comparison** - header subtitle shows "X tasks this week - Y% more/fewer than last week"
- **Period-over-period comparison** - when a date range is selected, charts overlay the equivalent prior period for comparison

---

## Insights & Trends

- **Insights Summary** - combined tile showing completion rates, project distribution, and weekly progress with trend indicators
- **Task Completion Trends** - a dedicated trend chart (separate from the raw completions-over-time chart) with comparison overlay

---

## Projects & Tasks

- **Completed Tasks by Project** - distribution of completed tasks by project (Dan has active tasks by project but not completed)
- **Backlog Health score** - a single health score derived from task age, overdue status, and high-priority tasks without due dates
- **Recurring Tasks** - dedicated tile tracking recurring task completion rates, streaks, and habit consistency
- **Recently Completed** - scrollable list of the most recently completed tasks

---

## Time & Pattern 

- **Task Lead Time Analysis** - full distribution chart of creation-to-completion time (Dan has only an avg-days KPI)

---

## Discovery

- **Task Topics / Word Cloud** - word cloud of the most common words across task names, revealing focus areas

---

## UX & Controls

- **Project filter** - filter the entire dashboard to one or more selected projects
- **Date range picker** - filter all charts to a custom date range or preset (7d, 30d, 90d, etc.)
- **Section visibility toggle** - show/hide individual dashboard sections
